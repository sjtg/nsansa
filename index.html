Introduction

We are a faith-based organization committed to working with vulnerable children and victims of different circumstances under the name Nsansa Village Community Development Mission Limited. We provide shelter, foodstuffs, and offer educational support to children.

NSANSA is a street children development program based in Lusaka, the capital city of Zambia. We are a faith based, non-profit organization. It started with a 24/7 prayer movement and a heart to help the poor children on the street in 2009.

23 Street children Boys were arrested by the police in 2016. The only way to set the freem was to provide them a facility to live. This was the beginning for Nsana and the care of first children.

The number of the children has increased to 60 Boys who live on site. Aged from 6 - 24 years. There are however still may who remain on the streets.

" To Rescue Street Children, restore lost Hope and lost dignity for the less privileged children in our society "

Objectives
  1. To identify and enroll children on the streets through a comprehensive targeting channels.
  2. To establish a secure safe home for the children.
  3. To give psychosocial support and counseling to children.
  4. To integrate children into the school education system and offer life skills training.

  2. Team work- Everyone’s contribution matters to sustain the mission statement.
