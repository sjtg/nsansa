About Us

We are a faith-based organization committed to working with vulnerable children and victims of different circumstances under the name Nsansa Village Community Development Mission Limited. We provide shelter, foodstuffs, and offer educational support to children.

We also provide; Training in life skills.

Mission Statement

To Rescue Street Children, restore lost Hope and lost dignity for the less privileged children in our society

Vision

To effectively provide a holistic service to the vulnerable children bringing them back into society as highly self-esteemed individuals.

Goal

Working with vulnerable children and victims of different circumstances. Then to restore human dignity and contribute to the improvement of their living standards.

Objectives
  1. To identify and enroll children on the streets through a comprehensive targeting channels.
  2. To establish a secure safe home for the children.
  3. To give psychosocial support and counseling to children.
  4. To integrate children into the school education system and offer life skills training.
Core Values
  1. Quality Service - We strive to give the best service to all victims.
  2. Team work- Everyone’s contribution matters to sustain the mission statement.
  3. Flexibility- We adjust the care we give to different children’s needs. However, we remain focused on our goal and mission.
  4. Innovation- We stay up to date with changes. Then offer an integrated approach to all issues.
What we do
Counselling

We journey with children who have experienced trauma on the streets. Through art, music and sport we want to build a healed community.

Feeding Program

We provide nutritious meals three times a day to 60 children. To sustain our growing village, it typically costs $400 per week. CHildren on the street are given food on our outreaches. On Sunday food is provided at Nsansa for all children after church.
