What We Do

We are a faith-based organization committed to working with vulnerable children and victims of different circumstances under the name Nsansa Village Community Development Mission Limited. We provide shelter, foodstuffs, and offer educational support to children.

By Promoting these Projects

OUR MISSION WILL BE ACCOMPLISHED

Education

The children living in Nsansa are given schooling. The teacher assesses the level each child is at and teaches them accordingly. Nsansa has accomplished further schooling at Chongwe. Once children have been counselled and it is felt that they can progress further. There is opportunity for the children to go to Chongwe a village 2 hour away from Lusaka. This is a boarding Primary school developed by Nsansa.

There is further building taking place which we hope to develop into a Secondary School Chongwe is in an ideal location, because they live in the school these children are more focused on their education therefore exam results are high.

Health for All Project

At Chongwe Nsansa works with the medical team assisting work at the rural clinic.

Nsansa gives Health Education to mothers and poor in Chongwe.

Nsansa provides a health outreach Door to Door to the people of Chongwe. Nsansa provides Medical Care for the Children on the streets. This is given by a Registered Nurse.

A good opportunity for foreign nurses/doctors to gain experience within Africa and Student Nurses to gain skills in emergency nursing by volunteering short or long-term. opportunities.
